# MFL Store

The MFL Store is where you can acquire brand new MFL assets. It contains assets that are redeemable using $MFL – the in-game currency that's used for rewards and can be earned through gameplay – as well as assets that are purchasable in cash.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (19).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Using $MFL

MFL Coin ($MFL) is the in-game currency, which you can earn by competing with your Club and/or signing your players to Contracts at Clubs owned by other owners. Learn more below.

### Reward Packs

In the MFL Store, you will find Reward Packs, which are single player packs that can only be purchased using $MFL. We review the price of these packs periodically to reflect the realities of the market, and they may fluctuate.

***

## Using Real Money

### New User Packs

Newly registered managers have packs reserved for them, to get the first players and even their first Club! These are available in the store for all eligible users.

### Player Pack Drops

During player pack drops, users have the opportunity to purchase a limited supply of packs that contain MFL Players, using real money. Pack contents vary depending on the type of pack purchased.&#x20;

To make things more exciting, luck also plays a part. A rare or legendary player could end up in any player slot. A common player slot, for example, has a 5% chance of containing an uncommon player, a 1% chance of containing a rare player, and a 0.2% chance of containing a legendary player.

**Player Pack Drops are usually announced well ahead of time, and last for as long as stocks do!**

{% hint style="success" %}
The thrill of getting your pack and discovering what’s inside is unmatched! We think the MFL pack-opening experience is just as exciting as ripping open a physical collectible pack. If you're lucky, you might land some Rare or Legendary players!
{% endhint %}
