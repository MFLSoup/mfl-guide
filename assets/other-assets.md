# Other Assets

There are two on-chain asset types in MFL at present: Players and Clubs. However, we are planning to expand the ecosystem with new and exciting tradeable items. These additions will introduce new dynamics to the game and give it more strategic depth.

Here is a brief rundown of the asset classes we will consider implementing in the future.

### Infrastructure _<mark style="color:yellow;">**\[Under Consideration]**</mark>_

* **Youth Academies** could give clubs the opportunity to shape a new generation of players.&#x20;
* **Stadiums** could become customizable, dynamic assets.
* **Training Facilities** could increase the quality, speed or frequency of training sessions.

### Personnel _<mark style="color:yellow;">**\[Under Consideration]**</mark>_

* _**A**_**gents** might deploy _**Scouts**_ to evaluate potential new recruits and add players to their portfolios.
* **Medical Staff** could be hired to look after the players' health, thus speeding up their recovery.
* **Specialist Coaches** could be brought in to help players improve or boost their performance in specific areas of the game ahead of future confrontations.

### Consumables _<mark style="color:yellow;">**\[Under Consideration]**</mark>_

**Consumables** could give players temporary performance or conditioning buffs. \
Skins, or similar assets that are purely cosmetic, may also be added to the ecosystem for extra customization options.

{% hint style="danger" %}
It is important to note that all of the above is a work-in-progress, and that we cannot, at this stage, guarantee that these features will ever be implemented. On the other hand, additional asset types that aren't on this list could eventually be deployed.\
\
Naturally, we will carefully consider any potential effects these changes may have on gameplay, game balance, and the economy.
{% endhint %}
