# Clubs

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Players v3 (2).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Clubs are central to the MFL ecosystem as they open up a world of opportunities for users and the players whose rights they own. Whether you own a club or not, you will need to interact with Club Owners in order to build relationships and negotiate deals. Ownership of a club, particularly in the top divisions, is a privilege that will keep users busy.

Each club will be able to field multiple teams, which they can enter in different competitions. In most cases, players can only be a part of a single team. We are yet to confirm the number of teams allowed per club.&#x20;

In this section, we will go over the basics of Clubs as an asset type.
