Club Owners also need to act as Managers and oversee their club's day-to-day operations. Users will be able to view any club's achievements, roster and contracts, competition rankings, and calendar.

This section focuses on two critical aspects of club management: training and tactics. \
Club owners will have to find the right balance between team performance and player development. Agents will want to keep a keen eye on their protégés' progress within their respective clubs, as both training and the manager's tactical choices will directly impact player growth.
