# Player Management

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Players v3 (5).png" alt="MFL Player management banner"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

MFL Players are dynamic assets, and their core attributes alone do not tell the whole story. Other variables come in to play, adding strategic depth and unpredictability to the ecosystem. A dedicated tab allow users to view the statistics, history, contractual situation, and other useful information for each MFL player.

Physical and mental conditioning drive changes in players' attributes on a daily basis, which in turn affects their performance and behavior.&#x20;

Some alterations, however, are permanent. Players evolve over the course of their careers, and it is on the user to make sure they can reach their full potential.
