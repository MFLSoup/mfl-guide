# Negotiations

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Players v3 (6).png" alt="MFL negotiations banner"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Transfer windows are particularly exciting times in MFL since all player-club transactions take place during that period.

As an Agent, it will be the perfect time to find a club that matches your players' aspirations and reach the most lucrative deals possible.\
Club Owners will be able to sign new players in a bid to fill the gaps in their rosters and achieve their objectives for the upcoming season.

{% hint style="info" %}
There are two Transfer windows: One during the offseason, and a shorter one around the halfway point of the season.
{% endhint %}
