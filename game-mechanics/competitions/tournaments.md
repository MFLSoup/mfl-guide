# Tournaments

## The IMFF Cup

The IMFF Cup is MFL's flagship Cup format, and the second of two official competitions that take place each season with no entry fees, where all clubs can participate – with the other being the League!

## Rapid Play and other Tournaments

Rapid Play tournaments are quick-fire competitions that take place during timed events, and cost an fee to enter. Put your $MFL on the line and fight for a share of the prize pool!

In the future, we're hoping to give you the opportunity to compete in all sorts of competitions, regardless of their budget or time commitment. That is why some competitions will have registration restrictions based on various factors, such as the following.

* **Age restrictions:** Some tournaments will have an age limit, where teams can only be composed of players under or over a certain age to join.&#x20;
* **Regional restrictions:** Some competitions will only be open to clubs in a specified geographical area. It might be a country (with national cup-style events) or a continent. (European clubs only, for example.)
* **Rarity restrictions:** Some tournaments will only allow squads made up of players from one or more defined rarity tiers.

{% hint style="success" %}
More official competitions may come to life in the future, like international competitions or continental club competitions!
{% endhint %}

## A word of caution

As long as you are eligible, you can enter your teams into as many tournaments as you like. However, make sure to keep your players' [conditioning ](../player-management/conditioning.md)in mind. Some tournaments will impact fitness and player development, while others won't. Overusing your players will lead to fatigue and, in turn, poor performance on the pitch.

{% hint style="info" %}
Users may eventually be able to create custom tournaments, but this will likely happen a little later down the line.
{% endhint %}
