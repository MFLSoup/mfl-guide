# Competitions

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Players v3 (4).png" alt="MFL competitions banner"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Club owners can enter their teams into various Leagues and Tournaments to earn rewards—and bragging rights. Prizes vary based on prestige, size, or length of the competition. In MFL, users will be able to view past and active competitions, their entrants, standings, and various statistics. Match reports will let users analyze previous performances.

Leagues are played through the entire season and consist of home and away matches between all of the entrants. Tournaments come in various formats and bring in the excitement of direct elimination.\
\
In this section, we will learn all about how to bring home the silverware and take a deep dive into the different formats of MFL competitions.&#x20;
